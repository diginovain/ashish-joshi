About Me

I build products,
not just features.

I'm a fullstack software engineer with a genuine eye for design. While most engineers hand off to a designer, I own the entire product loop — idea, wireframe, design system, frontend, backend, deployment.

My stack spans React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, FastAPI, and React Native. I design every screen in Figma before writing a line of code. That means no disconnect between design intent and implementation — what gets designed, gets built.

How I Work

My process, from
idea to launch.

01

Understand
Research the problem, the user, and the constraints. No wireframes until I know who I'm designing for and why.
02
✏️
Design
Every screen in Figma before a line of code. Wireframes, component libraries, responsive breakpoints, edge cases.
03
⚙️
Build
Fullstack development, top to bottom. Clean, modular code that faithfully implements the design — no drift.
